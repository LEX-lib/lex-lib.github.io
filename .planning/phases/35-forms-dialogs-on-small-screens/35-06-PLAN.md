1. iOS no-zoom (FD-01): On a real iPhone, open ManageExpense and tap the Amount field. The page must NOT zoom (stays at 1.0). (Devtools emulation cannot fully prove this — real device preferred; defer to Phase 38 if no device.)
2. Sticky action bar above keyboard (LT-08/FD-06): Open any Manage dialog as a bottom Drawer, tap a text field to open the keyboard. The Save/Cancel bar should remain visible/reachable; the focused field should scroll into view (center).
3. Camera two-affordance (FD-05): In ManageExpense / ManageMembership / ManageVaccination, confirm two buttons appear on mobile — "Take photo" (opens rear camera) and "Choose from gallery". Membership gallery is images-only; Expense/Vaccination gallery includes PDF.

5. Dirty guard (FD-09): Open a Manage dialog, change a field, then tap the backdrop / swipe down. A "Discard changes?" dialog should appear (Discard / Keep editing). Without changes, it should close immediately. Save and explicit Cancel must NOT prompt.
6. Invariants: In ManageMembership, the color picker still works and the saved color round-trips (edit, save, reopen — same color). In ManageVaccination Edit mode, the Date Administered field pre-fills with the record's date.
